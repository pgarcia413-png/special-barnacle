Personalized support for peace of mind

You focus on what matters, we'll handle the rest

Why Taluma?

Taluma is intentionally small, and that’s the point. You’re not handed off to layers of staff or treated like a ticket in a system. You get personal attention from a team that actually knows your books and your goals. We work closely with individuals, nonprofits, real estate businesses, and small businesses that need clarity, accuracy, and calm around their finances. The result is confidence: confidence in your numbers, in your compliance, and in your decisions. When tax time comes, there’s no panic scramble, just relief, because everything has already been handled with care, precision, and foresight. With Taluma, you can stop worrying about whether things are being done right and start trusting that your money is being taken care of.

What we offer

Bookkeeping Services

Taluma provides full-service bookkeeping designed to keep your financial records accurate, organized, and decision-ready. Services include day-to-day bookkeeping, financial record management, and regular reconciliations to ensure your numbers are always reliable. We offer QuickBooks setup, cleanup, and ongoing support, along with thoughtful chart of accounts design tailored to your business or organization. Taluma also supports accounts receivable and payable, invoicing, payroll coordination (excluding tax filings unless otherwise agreed), and the preparation of clear financial statements - so you always know where your money stands and can move forward with confidence.

Paralegal Support

Taluma’s paralegal services are structured to reduce administrative burden while supporting legal accuracy and compliance. We assist with form preparation as directed by you or your attorney, along with administrative and business support that helps keep operations running smoothly. These services are provided within a clearly defined scope and may be expanded through written agreement as needed. By combining meticulous organization with professional discretion, Taluma helps ensure that essential documentation and processes are handled efficiently, allowing you and your legal team to stay focused on what matters most.

Notary Services (California Only)

Taluma offers convenient notary services exclusively within the state of California (Los Angeles-based). We provide professional, reliable notarization to support your business and administrative needs, ensuring documents are executed properly and in compliance with California notary laws. Whether as a standalone service or in coordination with our bookkeeping or paralegal support, Taluma’s notary services add an extra layer of ease and confidence when finalizing important paperwork.
